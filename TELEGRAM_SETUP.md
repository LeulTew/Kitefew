    *   Send `/mybots` -> Select your bot.

    *   Send your **Vercel URL** (e.g., `https://kitefew.vercel.app`).
    *   Give it a label (e.g., "Play Now").
4.  **Done!** Users can now click "Play Now" in the bot to play.
